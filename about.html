About Our School

Discover our history, our values, and the strong academic foundation we provide for young minds.

Welcome to

Jai Kishan Shiksha Sansthan

Jai Kishan Shiksha Sansthan, established in 1999 in Ramnagar, Nainital, is deeply dedicated to providing high-quality, foundational education specifically tailored for young learners from Nursery to Class 5th.

As a primary-focused institution, we understand that a child's earliest years of schooling are the most critical. We pride ourselves on creating a localized, home-like environment where children feel secure, loved, and encouraged to explore their potential. With a highly affordable fee structure and dedicated teaching staff, we ensure that premium education is accessible to our community.

How We Teach

Our Pedagogy

We believe that learning should be a joyful experience, not a burden. Our teaching methods are designed to engage the curious minds of young children.

🧩

Experiential Learning

Children learn best by doing. We incorporate hands-on activities, educational games, and practical examples to make abstract concepts easy to grasp.

💖

Child-Centric Approach

Every child learns at their own pace. Our teachers focus on individual strengths, ensuring no student gets left behind in crowded classrooms.

🌱

Holistic Development

Beyond textbooks, we focus on emotional intelligence, physical well-being, and moral values to nurture well-rounded individuals.

What Sets Us Apart

Why Choose Us?

👩‍🏫 Experienced Educators

Our teachers are not just qualified; they are passionate about early childhood education and trained to handle the unique needs of primary students.

🛡️ Safe Campus

We provide a highly secure, localized environment. From secure entry gates to constant supervision, your child's safety is our #1 priority.

🤝 25% RTE Quota

We actively support the Right to Education Act, reserving 25% of our seats for children from the Economically Weaker Section (EWS).

💰 Affordable Fees

We strongly believe that quality education should not be a financial burden. Our fee structure is designed to be highly accessible for local parents.

Educational Structure

Our Academics

Pre-Primary (Nursery & KG)

Focuses on interactive play, basic motor skills, alphabet/number recognition, and social interaction in a highly supervised, fun environment.

Primary (Class 1 to 3)

Transitions into structured learning. Heavy emphasis is placed on reading fluency in Hindi and English, basic writing skills, and foundational arithmetic.

Upper Primary (Class 4 & 5)

Prepares students for middle school. Introduction to environmental studies, advanced grammar, and complex problem-solving abilities.

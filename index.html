Our Commitments

School Specialities

🎓

25% Seats Reserved (RTE)

In compliance with the Right to Education (RTE) Act, 25% of our seats are exclusively reserved for children from the Economically Weaker Section (EWS). We believe every child deserves quality education regardless of their financial background.

📚

Government Scholarships

We actively assist eligible families and students in processing documentation and applications to secure available Government Educational Scholarships, ensuring financial support reaches those who need it most.

🧸

Play-Way Methodology

For our foundational classes, we employ engaging play-way methods. Learning through educational toys, interactive games, and storytelling makes education a deeply joyful experience.

🎨

Co-Curricular Focus

We believe in holistic development. Our curriculum includes dedicated time for arts, crafts, basic physical education, and cultural activities to nurture hidden talents early on.
